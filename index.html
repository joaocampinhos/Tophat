Top hat

Top hat is the new css framework on the block. Created for rapid prototyping and focused on simplicity, without useless classes and styles that nobody will use.

Download

You can also download a precompiled version here.

Powered by SASS

With the SASS preprocessor, it's easy to create your own version of the framework without coding. Just change a couple of variables and you're good to go!

Modern Browser Suport

Top hat was created for modern web development and it supports modern browsers. From Chrome to Opera and even IE8! Isn't that awesome?

Responsive

With CSS media queries, coding for mobile devices is a breeze, and with a fluid responsive grid, it's a piece of cake to do mobile-first approaches.
